Overview

The Kicker Speed Control (KSC) is an electronic device designed to assist in precise idle speed control of kicker outboard motors, mainly used for trolling and fishing.

It allows you to adjust your boat‚Äôs speed easily, smoothly, and reproducibly, without constantly manipulating the engine throttle.

Adjustments can also be made through the Android application, which provides additional features for optimizing motor control. For more details, see the presentation document and the KSC App Manual.

Who is the KSC for?

  • Trolling anglers seeking precise speed control
  • Kicker motor users for slow navigation
  • People wanting more comfort during long outings

How it works

The KSC controls idle speed using a servo motor installed on the outboard engine.

Adjustments are made via a simple interface with a display and rotary encoder, allowing fine and progressive tuning.
