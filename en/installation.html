Installation

Kicker Speed Control (KSC) Installation Guide

Français | English

What’s in the Kit

The KSC kit includes all essential electronic components required for installation. Only basic tools and mounting hardware are needed to complete the setup.

  • 1. KSC Box
  • 2. KSC Stand with double-sided tape
  • 3. High-quality servo with in-line 3A step-down 5V voltage regulator
  • 4. Servo actuator attachments (to connect the servo to the carburetor)
  • 5. KSC power cable
  • 6. Power cable for servo and KSC Box
  • 7. Special linkage to connect the servo actuator to the motor carburetor

What You Need to Provide

  • Tools: Screwdriver, Allen key, wire cutter, wire stripper
  • Hardware: Wire, wire terminals, zip ties, electrical tape

1. Installation of the KSC Box

  • Choose a suitable location to install the KSC Box mounting stand.
  • Remove the protective tape from the stand and securely mount it.
  • Insert the KSC Box into the stand.
  • Use one of the two #6 cables provided in the kit (both are identical).
  • Connect the red wire to 12V positive, preferably powered when the boat dash master switch is ON.
  • Connect the green wire to 12V negative (ground).
  • Run the white wire to the white wire of the servo motor on the kicker (this wire must be provided by the installer).
  • WARNING: If the servo and the KSC Box are powered by different batteries, both batteries must share a common ground.
  • After connecting power, connect the #6 cable to the #5 cable provided in the kit, plug the USB-C into the KSC Box, and verify that it powers ON. Disconnect the USB-C after the test.

2. Installation of the Servo on the Kicker

  • Mounting the servo is often the most challenging part of the installation.
  • Servo installation kits are available online based on your kicker’s make, model, and year. The supplied brackets may not always fit perfectly, but parts of these kits are often useful.
  • Based on our experience, in more than 50% of installations the servo can be mounted directly to a metal bracket on the kicker motor aligned with the carburetor, secured using heavy-duty zip ties. (Pictures will be provided later.)
  • Connect the second #6 cable provided in the kit.
  • Connect the red wire to a constant 12V positive source (commonly the starter solenoid terminal). The servo draws no current, or extremely low current, when not actuated.
  • Connect the green wire to a solid ground on the kicker motor.
  • Connect the white wire to the white wire coming from the KSC Box.
  • Plug the connector into the servo motor.

3. Testing the KSC Box and Servo

  • Connect the USB-C cable to the KSC Box.
  • Connect the Android App to the KSC Box.
  • Press the MAX SPEED button and verify that the servo moves in the correct direction.
  • If the direction is incorrect, go to SETUP in the app and press Switch Rotation (CW / CCW). The KSC Box will restart and the app must be reconnected.
  • IMPORTANT: Never switch rotation when the servo actuator is connected to the carburetor. This step is only for initial installation and is saved in the KSC Box.

4. Linking the Servo Actuator to the Carburetor

  • Use parts from the installation kit specific to your kicker model if available.
  • The special #7 linkage included in the KSC kit can be used in many cases. It may require cutting to length and reshaping.
  • Always use the actuator hole closest to the servo shaft for the finest throttle adjustment. Trim excess length if using the nylon actuator.
  • Ensure the servo is set to 0 degrees and the kicker throttle is fully closed before linking the actuator.

5. Final Test with Engine Running

  • Start the kicker motor and be ready to shut it down immediately if needed.
  • With the engine idling, rotate the encoder knob on the KSC Box. The RPM should increase or decrease accordingly.
  • If the servo does not reach high idle or has too much travel:
    • Set the KSC Box to Speed 0.
    • Open SETUP in the app.
    • Select Max Angle and adjust as required.
    • Test again using the MAX SPEED button.

Enjoy Your KSC

Your KSC system is now ready to use. Don’t forget to watch the KSC Live Testing video and consult the KSC App Manual.
Good fishing!
