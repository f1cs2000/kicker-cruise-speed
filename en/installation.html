Installation

Kicker Speed Control (KSC) Installation Guide

Français | English

Preparation

  • Disconnect the motor’s power before any handling.
  • Check that all KSC components are present: display, encoder, ESP32, voltage regulator, servo, wiring, and installation kit.
  • Ensure you have the necessary tools to mount the servo in the engine compartment.

Servo Motor Installation

  • Mount the servo on the outboard motor using the installation kit for your specific model.
  • Ensure the servo is securely fixed and its arm can move freely without obstacles.
  • Check the mechanical connection between the servo and the idle control lever.

Electronics Connection

  • Connect the voltage regulator to the boat’s 12–14 V circuit.
  • Connect the ESP32 and display according to the provided wiring diagram.
  • Connect the servo to the KSC, respecting the correct polarity and wiring.

Initial Test

  • Before starting the motor, ensure all cables are secured.
  • Power on the KSC and observe the servo operation.
  • Make gradual idle adjustments to confirm everything is working correctly.
