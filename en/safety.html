Safety & Liability

Guidelines for safe use of the Kicker Speed Control (KSC)

Français | English

Important Safety Information

  • Always disconnect power before installing or servicing the KSC.
  • Ensure the servo and electronics are securely mounted to avoid movement or damage.
  • Do not modify wiring or components unless you are qualified and understand the electronics.
  • Keep the device dry and protected from water intrusion.

Liability

The Kicker Speed Control (KSC) is provided as a tool to assist in precise idle speed control of small outboard motors. Use is at your own risk. The manufacturer and distributor are not responsible for damages or accidents resulting from improper installation, misuse, or modifications.

Recommendations

  • Follow all installation instructions carefully.
  • Test the system in calm conditions before regular use.
  • Keep children and pets away from the motor during operation.
